Welcome!

At the Purrfect Cup, we blend two comforting favorites: a cozy cup of coffee and the companionship of cats.
Every visit and donation helps support our efforts to provide the care and love our feline friends deserve. Whether you come in to relax, adopt, or enjoy some company, you're helping make a difference, and we greatly appreciate it!
Please stop by, take a seat, and find your purrfect friend!
