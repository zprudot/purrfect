Our Mission

We proudly aim to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats who didn't get the opportunity to do so. We will create a warm and welcoming space for people and cats to interact and connect. We encourage compassion and will provide opportunities for cats to find their forever home through adoption and community support. Every purchase and visit helps fund our rescue goals, veterninary care, and adoption program.

Our Services

  1. Cat adoption service and guidance.
  2. Meeting sessions with adoptable felines.
  3. Educational workshops for cat care and advocacy.
  4. Volunteer opportunities to gain experience and help care for rescues.
  5. A welcoming, cat-friendly space for interaction.
  6. Freshly brewed coffee and espresso.
  7. Hosting private events and small gatherings.
